Our Story

A small kitchen, three generations of recipes.

Rohini Foods India began in a modest home kitchen, where the smell of sun-dried mangoes and mustard seeds would drift through every summer afternoon. What started as jars made for family and neighbours has, over the years, grown into something our customers across India look forward to.
